FAQ

A few practical answers before we talk.

Do you work with templates?

For this demo: no templates, just clean components. For real client work, we can build bespoke or adapt to your platform if there's a good reason.

Can we start with just a landing page?

Yes. It's often the best first step. If the landing converts and the offer is clear, we expand from a place of proof instead of hope.

What do you need from me?

A clear decision maker, access to existing analytics (if any), and a willingness to cut anything that isn't pulling its weight.

What do you build with?

This site is a static HTML/CSS/JS build. For production, we choose the stack based on your needs: static, CMS, or storefront.

Want a recommendation?

Tell me what you're selling and what's currently not working. I'll recommend a starting package and the simplest next step.
