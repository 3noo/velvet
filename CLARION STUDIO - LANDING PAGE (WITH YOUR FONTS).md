## **TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM**

### **Font 1: Meno Banner \- Display Headlines**

**Use for:** Large section headlines, hero text, major statements  
 **Style:** Elegant, editorial, high-impact  
 **Color:** Warm Charcoal `#3D3633` or Deep Terracotta `#C1704F`  
 **Examples where used:**

* "Recent work"  
* "Studio offerings"  
* "How we work together"  
* Hero section main headline

### **Font 2: Sofia Pro \- Body Text & Subheadings**

**Use for:** All paragraph text, descriptions, smaller headlines, navigation  
 **Style:** Clean, modern, readable, warm  
 **Weight:** Regular for body, Medium for emphasis, Bold for small headlines  
 **Color:** Warm Charcoal `#3D3633`  
 **Examples where used:**

* Story section paragraphs  
* Service descriptions  
* Navigation menu  
* All body copy

### **Font 3: Allura (or Southam Demo) \- Handwritten Accents**

**Use for:** Quotes, personal notes, signature elements, annotations  
 **Style:** Script/handwritten, elegant but readable  
 **Color:** Deep Terracotta `#C1704F` or Burnt Sienna `#A0522D`  
 **Examples where used:**

* The signature quote section  
* Small notes like "Based in Barcelona"  
* Personal touches and annotations  
* "The Clarion Method" tagline

### **Font 4: Sofia Pro Medium/Bold \- Buttons & Labels**

**Use for:** Call-to-action buttons, tags, small labels  
 **Style:** Sans-serif, clean, modern  
 **Color:** Cream (on terracotta buttons) or Warm Charcoal  
 **Examples where used:**

* All buttons ("Book a Call", "View Services", etc.)  
* Navigation menu items  
* Category labels

# **TECHNICAL NOTES FOR DEVELOPER**

**Fonts to load:**

* Meno Banner (Regular weight)  
* Sofia Pro (Regular, Medium, Bold, Regular Italic)  
* Allura (Regular weight)

**Animations:**

* Smooth fade/slide transitions on carousels (3-4 sec intervals)  
* Horizontal auto-scroll on testimonials (slow, continuous)  
* Background video loop (muted, subtle)  
* Fade-in on scroll (subtle, not jarring)

**Mobile responsive:**

* Stack all side-by-side layouts vertically  
* Reduce font sizes proportionally (about 70-80% of desktop)  
* Maintain readability and touch-friendly buttons
